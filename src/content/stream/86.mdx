Today I learned how to also show the time in [[mu4e]]'s email list.

This is how you do it:

```elisp
(setq mu4e-headers-date-format "%Y-%m-%d %H:%M")

(setq mu4e-headers-fields
  '((:human-date . 18) ; this needs to be there
    (:flags . 6)
    (:from . 22)
    (:subject)))
```

Sweet!
