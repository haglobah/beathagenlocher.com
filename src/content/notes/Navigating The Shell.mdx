Programming-wise, one of the things that stayed a mystery for me the longest was how to navigate a shell effectively and efficiently.

I knew the very basics from relatively early on (my dad had an old MS-DOS PC), but my skills didn't really improve I until after I'd learned to use my first few [[Programming Language]]s effectively. That was way later than it needed to be.

And as it turns out with most things programming—it's not even that _hard_, just _a lot to learn_.  
But you get rewarded handsomely: You get lots of [Wizard Power]() in return.

So, hoping that I've motivated this enough—this should be a good starting point:
